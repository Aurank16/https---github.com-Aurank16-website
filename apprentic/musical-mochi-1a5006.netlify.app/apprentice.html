Apprentice

Apprentice is a service that focuses on providing organizations with skilled workers trained on-the-job to meet their specific needs. This program offers an opportunity for individuals to learn new skills through hands-on experience while also allowing employers to train and develop their workforce. This service helps organizations address the skills gap and improve their productivity while providing apprentices with valuable work experience and training.

NATS SCHEME

The National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) is an initiative launched in April 2022 to promote apprenticeship training. It offers on-the-job training with incentives for companies, contributing to their CSR efforts, and involves no statutory obligations.

BACKGROUND

The Apprentices Act, 1961, was established with the goal of providing on-the-job training as one of the most efficient ways to develop skilled manpower, without imposing the burden of training infrastructure on companies.

Eligibility: The scheme is applicable to trainees holding a Diploma, Graduate, or higher qualifications.

NAPS Scheme

The National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) is an initiative aimed at enhancing apprenticeship training, launched in December 2018. It offers on-the-job training opportunities with various benefits for companies, including CSR advantages and no statutory obligations.

BACKGROUND

The Apprentices Act, 1961, was established to provide on-the-job training, recognized as one of the most efficient methods for developing skilled manpower without the burden of establishing extensive training infrastructure.

The scheme is applicable for trainees with educational qualifications ranging from the 5th grade up to ITI Diploma holders.
